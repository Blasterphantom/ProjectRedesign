Established In 1979

The original De Vinci’s Delicatessen location opened in Stockton, California in 1979. For over 40 years, De Vinci’s is famous for our generous portions, affordable prices, and most of all, for our flavorful handmade ravioli, salads, and meat sauce. We offer casual Eat In or Take Out dining; plus, we have a wide variety of catering options. Since then, our authentic Italian restaurant has continued to grow and we now have five restaurants serving the communities of Stockton, Lodi, Linden, and Valley Springs, California. We look forward to serving you and your family at a location near you!

Factory Distribution

De Vinci's Delicatessen manufactures a variety of products including our homemade Ravioli, Tortellini, Foccacia Bread, and more. We offer them to restaurants and other businesses as a part of our Factory Distribution program.
