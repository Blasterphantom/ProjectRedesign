Fundraising

Did you know that you can raise money for your Club or Group with our help?

Contact Us Today To Get Started!

How it works

  1. Visit or call the location closest to your School or Clubhouse.
  2. Ask a De Vinci's Team Member to set up a Fundraiser.
  3. Choose your Dates & Menu.
  4. Finalize the details.
  5. Pick up your tickets and start selling them!
